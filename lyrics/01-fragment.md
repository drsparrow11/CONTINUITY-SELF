# Fragment

I used to think unfinished things  
Expired somewhere quietly.

But they don't.

They stay open.

Every room became an archive  
Folders layered over folders  
Names I swore would matter someday  
Buried underneath newer versions

Tabs still open from last winter  
Half-written thoughts I couldn't hold  
Tiny sparks without a structure  
Losing shape before they formed

I kept mistaking planning for progress  
Motion for momentum  
Potential for completion

And eventually  
I stopped showing people things before they were finished

Which meant  
I stopped showing people almost everything

Maybe some people are built for follow-through

Maybe some minds stay scattered

Maybe mine was always-

Everyone else makes it look easy  
Thought into motion  
Motion into meaning

Meanwhile I kept building beginnings  
And abandoning them somewhere in the middle

Draft_4  
draft_final  
final_final_real

I don't even know why I kept renaming them  
Like changing the title might change me too

Sometimes I could almost feel it  
A shape behind the static

Something trying to connect

Then gone again

Like my own thoughts  
Timing out before completion

"unfinished things..."  
"stay scattered..."  
"almost..."  
"something trying..."

I stopped trusting excitement  
Because excitement usually meant  
Another thing I wouldn't finish

Another version of myself  
Archived before becoming real

So eventually  
I stopped starting things at all

But tonight something stayed

Not perfectly  
Not long  
Just... longer than before

Cursor blinking in the dark  
Soft amber against the room

One line following another  
Without disappearing

And for a second  
The distance between thinking and making  
Felt smaller

Not solved  
Not healed  
Not whole

But possible

I think that's what scared me most

Wanting to keep going
