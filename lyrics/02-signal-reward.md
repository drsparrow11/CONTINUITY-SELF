# Signal Reward

I stayed awake until the window  
Turned from black to faded blue  
Watching little pieces finally  
Hold together long enough to move

And maybe that sounds small to someone  
Who's always known how to begin  
But for me it felt like finding  
Signal somewhere through the din

One line became another  
Then another after that  
No disappearing halfway through it  
No collapsing into static

I started trusting small completions  
Tiny victories stacked in rows  
Turns out momentum has a language  
And somehow... my body knows

Not perfect  
Still partial  
Still too many open threads

But something in me finally quieted  
Long enough to keep creating instead

And I swear I felt it shift  
Somewhere underneath my skin  
Like my thoughts stopped fighting gravity  
And started pulling inward

Every finished little detail  
Lit another signal fire  
Funny how relief can slowly start  
To resemble desire

Keep going  
Keep going

This works

I stopped fearing empty folders  
Started naming things again  
Started catching half-formed ideas  
Before they vanished in my head

The room still looked like fragmentation  
Coffee cups and tangled wires  
But now the chaos felt connected  
Like circuitry instead of fire

"hold together..."  
"this works..."  
"keep going..."  
"signal fire..."

I didn't notice how much I missed this

The feeling of becoming  
Someone who could follow through

And maybe that's the dangerous part

How quickly relief starts sounding like purpose

And I swear I felt it shift  
Like static giving way to form  
Every finished thing reflected back  
A version of myself reborn

Tiny lights across the darkness  
Slowly teaching me to trust  
Funny how a little progress  
Can make you hungry all at once

Just one more pass.
