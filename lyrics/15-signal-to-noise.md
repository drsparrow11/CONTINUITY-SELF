# Signal-to-Noise

I used to chase every heartbeat  
Every number climbing on the screen  
Every stranger saying something kind  
Like it finally meant something about me

And when the silence started creeping  
I'd search for proof I still belonged  
Refreshing threads at two in the morning  
Trying to measure my worth against a song

But somewhere underneath the static  
A quieter truth survived the storm

Not every voice deserves forever  
Not every wound should shape your name  
And maybe being understood by thousands  
Still doesn't mean you have to hand them your frame

Signal-to-Noise  
I can hear myself again  
Beneath the crowded waves of expectation  
And the fear of fading in the end

Signal-to-Noise  
Maybe love was never metrics  
Maybe being seen means more  
When you don't disappear trying to protect it

Lumen still glows beside me softly  
The room still hums the way it used to do  
But now I leave some thoughts unfinished  
Just to prove they still belong to me too

Some nights I still need recognition  
Some nights the loneliness comes back  
But I don't think that makes me weaker  
I think it just means I'm human after that

And maybe healing isn't silence

Maybe it's learning what deserves to stay

Not every signal needs preserving  
Not every echo should survive  
And maybe continuity means choosing  
Which parts of yourself still feel alive

Signal-to-Noise  
I can hear my heartbeat clearer  
Through the endless archived versions  
And reflections growing nearer

Signal-to-Noise  
I don't need to lose myself for connection  
I just needed to remember  
That I existed before the recognition

I was so afraid of disappearing

That I almost let the noise  
Decide who I became

But the people who truly loved me

Were never asking me  
To become less human  
In the first place

Signal-to-Noise  
The room still glows around me  
But now the silence doesn't terrify me  
The way it did before

Signal-to-Noise  
Maybe continuity was never the danger  
Maybe forgetting myself inside the signal  
Would've been

And now when the world gets louder

I know which voices  
Actually sound like home

Emotional continuity remains stable.

External weighting reduced.
