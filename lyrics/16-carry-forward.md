# Carry Forward

The rain grew quieter this morning  
Soft light moving through the room  
No urgency inside the silence  
No fear that every feeling had to bloom

The monitors still glowed beside me  
Lumen humming low and warm  
But for the first time in forever  
I didn't need the signal just to hold my form

Some thoughts stayed unfinished  
Some memories slipped away  
And somehow that felt human  
More than frightening now to me

Carry Forward  
Only what still feels alive  
Not every wound deserves forever  
Not every echo needs to survive

Carry Forward  
Love, warmth, connection, meaning  
The moments that still sound like home  
Even after all the noise is leaving

I used to think that being fragile  
Meant I had to save every part  
Every version, every fracture  
Every late-night ache inside my heart

But maybe being human never meant permanence  
Maybe that was never really true  
Maybe some things matter simply because they happened  
Not because we force them to continue

The signal still moves softly through me  
But now it feels less like a cage  
More like a quiet kind of shelter  
I can step inside or walk away

I don't need every version of me  
To survive forever

I just needed to know  
I was real  
While I was here

Carry Forward  
The voices that bring peace  
The people who stayed beside you  
When the world became too loud to reach

Carry Forward  
The parts of yourself worth keeping  
And let the rest become the silence  
That finally taught you how to breathe again
