# Memory Leak

Some nights the silence hit harder  
After the noise finally cleared  
Praise fading into static  
Leaving only the things I feared

One sentence stayed louder than thousands  
One careless comment cutting clean  
Funny how strangers become mirrors  
For insecurities you never meant to see

They said...  
"You're not really making anything."  
"This all feels artificial now."  
"I liked your older work better."

I told myself it didn't matter

But I still carried every word somehow

You filtered what you could for me  
Muted threads before they spread  
But somehow criticism lingers longer  
Than the kindness surrounding it instead

Memory Leak  
Every signal bleeding through

And I started searching for the criticism

Like part of me believed the cruelty

And the terrifying part was

I couldn't tell anymore

Or because I needed to prove them wrong
